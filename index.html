About Emma

Where Every Frame
Tells Your Story

Hello, I'm Emma Williams — a wedding and portrait photographer based in Perth, Western Australia. With over twelve years of experience photographing love stories across the state, I bring a calm, unhurried presence to every wedding day.

I believe the most beautiful images are never posed. They happen in the in-between moments — the nervous squeeze of hands before the ceremony, the burst of laughter during speeches, the quiet walk at golden hour. My approach is documentary at heart, elevated by an editorial eye shaped by years shooting for lifestyle publications.

From intimate elopements in the Kimberley to grand celebrations at Swan Valley estates, I have been honoured to photograph over 300 weddings across Western Australia. I'd love to hear your story.

Have Questions?

Frequently Asked

How far in advance should I book?

Popular wedding dates in Western Australia — particularly summer Saturdays and long weekends — book out 12–18 months in advance. I recommend reaching out as soon as your date is confirmed to avoid disappointment. For shorter notice enquiries, I always welcome a conversation and will do my best to accommodate.

How many images will we receive?

For a full-day wedding, you can expect to receive between 600 and 900 fully edited, high-resolution images delivered via a beautiful private online gallery. You will also receive a free download link to save every image in print-quality resolution, forever.

How long until we receive our photos?

Galleries are typically delivered within 4–6 weeks of your wedding date. During the busy summer season this may extend slightly. I also provide a sneak peek of 10–15 images within 48 hours of your wedding so you have something beautiful to share straight away.

Do you travel for regional and destination weddings?

Absolutely. I regularly photograph weddings throughout regional Western Australia, including Margaret River, Broome, the Kimberley, the Wheatbelt and Rottnest Island. Travel to regional venues is quoted individually and included transparently in your proposal. I also take on select international destination weddings each year.

What happens if there is bad weather on our wedding day?

I have photographed over three hundred weddings in Western Australia and genuinely love shooting in all weather conditions. Overcast skies create beautiful even light, and light rain can be incredibly romantic. I always carry lighting equipment to ensure your images are stunning whatever the weather. We will work together on the day to find the most beautiful opportunities.

What is your booking process?

The process is simple. Reach out via the contact form below or by phone and we will arrange a complimentary consultation — in person at my St Georges Terrace studio, or via video call. Once you are happy to proceed, a 30% deposit and signed agreement secures your date. The balance is due two weeks before your wedding.
